## ⚠️ Common Mistakes - DON'T Do These

❌ **Starting with Ethereum mainnet**
- Too expensive ($150 vs $30)
- Too high gas costs
- Only for experts

❌ **Deploying to zkEVM early**
- No Aave V3 (can't do flash loans easily)
- Low volume
- Wait 6 months

❌ **Trying BSC first**
- Needs custom development
- More complex than others
- Do AFTER you have 3 chains working

❌ **Deploying all at once without testing**
- Can't debug which has issues
- Overwhelming
- Deploy 1-2, test, then continue

❌ **Skipping observation periods**
- Need to validate each works
- 24-48 hours minimum per chain
- Prevents compounding issues
